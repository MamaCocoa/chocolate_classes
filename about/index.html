Our Mission - Bring smiles to every face we meet.

Whether we are teaching classes or producing confections, every day our mission is to bring smiles to every face we meet. We deliver high-quality, hand-made confections, with carefully-sourced ingredients and earth-wise packaging. We want to perpetuate the craft that makes people smile. We are just as passionate about teaching our techniques as we are about making quality products.

So if you ever tasted our products, you know how much we love to teach you about topics related to making those products. If you aren't interested in recipe development or even eating chocolate, we offer other classes on caramel recipes and techniques and the history and origins of confections. We appreciate our customers and students and want to continue to spread joy for many years to come.

Contact us, for chocolate's sake.
