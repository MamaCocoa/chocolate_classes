Milk Chocolate Turtle Brix:

Recipe developed by Julie Farrell, Tease Chocolates, Brookfield, Wisconsin.

Turtle Brix is gooey pecan caramel hand-dipped in Milk Chocolate or Dark Chocolate with Sea Salt. Each batch has 3 lbs. of whole roasted pecans. Julie packages them in a fitted cello bag, 2 pieces per package and seals it with a detailed, FDA approved ingredients label and a branded sticker. Julie says, "It's soon to be your favorite!"
