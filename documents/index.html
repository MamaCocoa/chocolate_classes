Milk Chocolate Trutle Brix

Prep Time: 30 Min

Cook Time: 60 Min

Total Time: 90 Min

If you like to cook caramel you will love this simple classic peacan caramel recipe. Microwave tempering your chocolate is a quick and fool-proof method. They make a great snack or can be embellished as a dessert. They also make a great hostess gift. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1-1/2 cups roasted pecns
  • 16 oz. microwave tempered Milk Chcolate

Directions

  1. Line 8x8 pan with plastic wrap. Set aside.
  2. Add first 4 ingredients in deep saucepan or dutch oven. Cook on medium-high heat stirring constantly.
  3. Cook to 238° F. Add nuts and immediately pour into prepared pan.
  4. When caramel is completely cooled, cut into 2 inch bricks.
  5. Use a large 3-pronged dipping fork to dip each piece in Milk Chocolate.
